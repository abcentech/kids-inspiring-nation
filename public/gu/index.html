✶ Understanding the gPA System
What is the gPA?
The goD Points Average tracks how consistently your child engages with their spiritual curriculum each week. Not a test score — it measures habit, commitment and presence in God’s Word across 44 weeks.
📋
Weekly Report
A written reflection each week on what was studied. Builds documentation and accountability habits every nation-builder needs.
⏱️
Time Sheet
Daily log of reading sessions. If you cannot measure it, you cannot grow it.
📖
Pathway (Word Challenge)
Completion of assigned Bible chapters — OT, NT, or Psalms & Proverbs. The core spiritual curriculum.
🎯
Activity
Class discussions, parables, memory work and exercises. Active participation shows genuine engagement.
📡
Attendance
Showing up on time, fully present, camera on. The foundation — everything else builds on it.
⌚
Invested Minutes (tracked separately — not scored)
Total minutes in God’s Word each week. Target: 300+ min/week. Shown alongside the gPA but not included in the % calculation.
Scoring Key
1.0 = Fully Done
0.5 = Partially Done
0 = Not Done
Minutes = Raw time (not scored)
gPA % = average of 5 scored categories.
80%+ = Excellent  ·  60–79% = Good  ·  40–59% = Fair  ·  <40% = Needs Attention
